POPULAR DESTINATION IN ETHIOPIA

ADDIS ABABA & SURROUNDING

As a chartered city, Addis Ababa serves as the capital city of Ethiopia. It is where the African Union is headquartered and where its predecessor the Organization of African Unity (OAU) was based. It also hosts the headquarters of the United Nations Economic Commission for Africa, as well as various other continental and international organizations.

ADVENTURAL & COFFEE TOURS

Mainly known for Rainforests & coffee plantations share the landscape with savannah grasslands and includes coffee areas like Limu Kaffa, Jimma, Illubabor, Wellega and others. There was previously some other washed coffees in Limu, where the state owned farm Kossa started with washed coffees about 30 years ago. It is also ideal place for coffee tours.

NORTHERN HISTORIC ROUTES

Northern Ethiopia can be divided into two geographical regions: the highlands to the west, and containing Ethiopia's highest peak at Ras Dashen, and the Afar lowlands to the east, containing the country's lowest point in the Danakil Depression. The Ethiopian Highlands are very different from what one might imagine when thinking of East Africa.

POPULAR DESTINATION AROUND THE WORLD

Amsterdam

Amsterdam, the vibrant capital of the Netherlands, is a city of picturesque canals, historic architecture, and rich cultural heritage. Known for its world-renowned museums, charming streets, and bustling markets, Amsterdam offers a unique blend of old-world charm and modern sophistication. Whether you're cruising along its iconic waterways, visiting the Anne Frank House, or enjoying the lively atmosphere of its cafes and nightlife, Amsterdam promises an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

Sydney

Sydney, Australia's stunning harbor city, captivates visitors with its iconic landmarks, beautiful beaches, and vibrant culture. From the architectural marvel of the Sydney Opera House and the historic Sydney Harbour Bridge to the golden sands of Bondi Beach, Sydney offers an unparalleled mix of natural beauty and urban sophistication. Stroll through the lush Royal Botanic Garden, explore the diverse neighborhoods, or indulge in world-class dining and shopping experiences.

Dubai

Dubai, a dazzling jewel in the UAE, is renowned for its ultramodern architecture, luxurious shopping, and vibrant nightlife. Home to the towering Burj Khalifa, the opulent Burj Al Arab, and the sprawling Dubai Mall, this cosmopolitan city blends tradition with innovation. Wander through the historic Al Fahidi neighborhood, marvel at the Palm Jumeirah, or embark on a thrilling desert safari. With Andentur Travels, immerse yourself in the extravagance and cultural richness of Dubai and more.
